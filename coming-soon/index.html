Yes. Each lab spins up an isolated Linux environment. You type real commands, they run on real Linux. Not a fake shell or text parser — actual bash on actual Linux.

No. Nothing. Open winlab.cloud in any modern browser and start. Works on Mac, Windows, Linux, even iPad.

It watches your session and gives contextual hints — like "you're on the right track, try looking at the file permissions." It never just gives you the answer, because that would kill the learning. Think of it as a senior sysadmin watching over your shoulder.

Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel from your dashboard or email hello@winlab.cloud. No questions asked, no retention loop. You lose access at the end of your billing period.
