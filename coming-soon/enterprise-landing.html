Enterprise features

Everything a serious team needs

From SSO to tenant isolation to custom scenarios — built for IT teams that train at scale.

🔐

SSO / SAML 2.0

Integrate with Azure AD, Okta, or Google Workspace. Users log in with their corporate credentials — no new passwords to manage.

🏢

Tenant isolation

Your team's data, progress, and custom scenarios are completely isolated. No cross-tenant data exposure.

👥

Role-based access

Assign DevOps, Security, IT Manager, or Admin roles. Each role sees relevant scenarios and has appropriate permissions.

📊

Admin dashboard

See completion rates, skill gaps, leaderboard rankings, and time-to-resolve metrics for every member of your team.

🤖

AI Mentor (team mode)

Contextual guidance during scenarios. The AI adapts to each user's skill level and flags common mistakes to the admin.

📈

Adaptive difficulty

Scenarios automatically adjust difficulty based on performance history. Junior engineers and senior engineers get appropriate challenges.

📄

PDF report export

Generate professional scenario reports for each session. Useful for compliance documentation, performance reviews, and board presentations.

🧩

Custom scenarios

Build scenarios specific to your environment — your tooling, your architecture, your runbooks. Available on Enterprise plan.

API access

Integrate WinLab with your existing LMS, Jira, Slack, or Teams. Webhooks available for scenario completions and alerts.
