## 💡 Pro Tips for X

1. **First 3 seconds matter most** — Hook must grab attention immediately
2. **Square videos get 2x engagement** vs landscape on X
3. **Silent autoplay** — No audio needed, text tells the story
4. **Post during commute hours** — 8-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM CET
5. **Use 3-5 hashtags max** — #sysadmin #devops #learning #tech #career
6. **Engage with replies** — Algorithm favors active conversations
7. **Pin top performer** — After 48h, pin best video to profile
8. **Thread the series** — Create "Day 1/20" thread format
9. **Add polls** — "Have you experienced this?" boosts engagement
10. **Cross-pollinate** — Reply to relevant tweets with video links
