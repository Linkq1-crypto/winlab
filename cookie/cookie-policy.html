Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how WinLab ("we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on winlab.cloud.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience. Some cookies are essential for the site to function; others help us understand how visitors use the site.

3. Cookie Categories

Essential cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They include authentication tokens, cookie consent preferences, Cloudflare security cookies, and Stripe payment cookies. They do not require your consent under GDPR/ePrivacy.

Analytics cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with WinLab by collecting anonymized usage data. They are only set if you explicitly consent via the cookie banner. Currently this includes the winlab_plan cookie used by our first-party analytics system.

4. Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use the browser's sendBeacon API to send anonymized analytics events (page views, feature usage) to our servers. This only activates when you have consented to analytics cookies. No data is sent to third-party analytics providers.

5. How to Manage Cookies

Via our cookie banner

When you first visit WinLab, you can accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the shield icon (🛡) in the bottom-left corner of any page.

Via your browser

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Please note that blocking essential cookies may prevent WinLab from functioning correctly.
