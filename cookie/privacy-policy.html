2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account registration: email address, password (hashed)
  • Waitlist signup: email address
  • Payment information: processed entirely by Stripe, Inc. We do not store credit card numbers, CVVs, or full card details on our servers. We receive from Stripe: customer ID, subscription status, and last 4 digits of your card.
  • Lab interactions: commands you type during training labs, which are processed by our AI Mentor feature
  • Support communications: any emails or messages you send us

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage analytics: page views, session duration, feature usage — collected via a first-party, privacy-focused analytics system (no third-party tracking pixels). This data is sent using the browser's sendBeacon API and stored on our servers.
  • Technical data: IP address (anonymized), browser type, operating system, device type, referral source
  • Cookies: see our Cookie Policy for details

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • To process your subscription and payments
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, payment receipts)
  • To power the AI Mentor feature within labs (your lab inputs are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for real-time assistance)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the Service
  • To communicate with you about updates, if you opted in
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): processing necessary to provide the Service you subscribed to
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): for analytics cookies and optional marketing communications. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): for fraud prevention, security, and Service improvement

  • The EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (where applicable)

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active, and for 30 days after deletion
  • Waitlist data: retained until you unsubscribe or the waitlist is closed
  • Payment records: retained for 10 years as required by Italian tax law
  • Analytics data: aggregated and anonymized after 26 months
  • AI Mentor interactions: not stored permanently; processed in real-time and discarded

8. Your Rights

8.1 Rights under GDPR (EEA residents)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la protezione dei dati personali) or your local supervisory authority

8.2 Rights under CCPA (California residents)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it is used
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information — we do not sell your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

8.3 How to exercise your rights

Send a request to privacy@winlab.cloud. We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA). To delete your account, you can also use the "Delete Account" function within the platform.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including: HTTPS encryption (via Cloudflare), JWT tokens in httpOnly cookies, password hashing (bcrypt), rate limiting, and regular security reviews. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. Children's Privacy

WinLab is not directed at individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@winlab.cloud.
