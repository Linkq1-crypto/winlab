AI Mentor uses a third-party service

WinLab's AI Mentor is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. To provide real-time guidance during labs, the commands and questions you type are sent to Anthropic's servers for processing.

↑
What is sent: your lab commands, questions, and error messages — in the context of the current lab session only
✓
Not used for training: Anthropic does not use API data to train their models
✓
Not stored permanently: data is processed in real-time and not retained by Anthropic beyond 30 days
⇄
Where: Anthropic is based in the United States. Transfer is covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)

You can use all labs without the AI Mentor. If you decline, you'll still have access to every lab scenario — just without AI-powered hints and guidance.

You can change this anytime in Settings. Read our Privacy Policy.
