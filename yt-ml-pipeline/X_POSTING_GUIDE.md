## Pro Tips for Maximum Reach

### Thread Structure That Works:
1. **Hook tweet** (with video) - MUST be surprising/controversial
2. **Value tweets** (1/, 2/, 3/) - Actionable, specific tips
3. **CTA tweet** - Clear next step (follow, visit, buy)

### Best Practices:
✅ Post at **exact** peak hours (8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 6-8 PM)
✅ Use **🧵 emoji** in first tweet (signals thread)
✅ Number your points (1/, 2/, 3/) - easier to read
✅ End with **question** (drives replies)
✅ Reply to **every comment** (boosts algorithm)
✅ **Video attached** to first tweet (more engagement)
✅ **Tag relevant accounts** (if applicable)
✅ Use **1-2 hashtags** max (not spammy)

❌ Don't post after 10 PM (low engagement)
❌ Don't use generic hooks ("Check this out")
❌ Don't skip follow-up tweets
❌ Don't ignore comments (algorithm penalty)
❌ Don't use more than 3 hashtags
❌ Don't post threads without video
